About the game

Your goal is to destroy enemy helicopters, which are controlled by other players. In your arsenal there are bullets and rockets. Use them to destroy enemy helicopters. In the game randomly appear first aid kits that will help you if your lifes is running out. You can also destroy their, so that the enemy player can not use them.

Try it!

Сontrol

  • W - move up;
  • A - move left;
  • S - move down;
  • D - move right;
  • SPACE - open fire with bullets;
  • SHIFT - open fire with rockets.
